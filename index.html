About Us

Welcome to Calm Africa Safaris

Welcome to Calm Africa Safaris, your gateway to unforgettable adventures across East Africa. We specialize in creating tailor-made safari experiences that cater to your unique needs and preferences. Whether you're looking to explore the vast savannas of Kenya, the lush forests of Uganda, the stunning landscapes of Tanzania, or the diverse wildlife of Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of Congo, we have the perfect safari for you.

Our dedicated team of experienced and professional tour guides is committed to providing you with a memorable and enriching experience. With our well-maintained fleet of comfortable safari vehicles, you can be assured of a safe and enjoyable journey. We offer a variety of safari packages that are available all year round, allowing you to book your dream adventure at any time.

At Calm Africa Safaris, we believe in delivering a wholesome and immersive experience of East Africa. Join us and discover the beauty, culture, and wildlife that this incredible region has to offer. Your adventure of a lifetime starts here.

Why visit Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa whose diverse landscape encompasses the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains and immense Lake Victoria. Its abundant wildlife includes chimpanzees as well as rare birds. Remote Bwindi Impenetrable National Park is a renowned mountain gorilla sanctuary. Murchison Falls National Park in the northwest is known for its 43m-tall waterfall and wildlife such as hippos.
