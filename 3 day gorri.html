3 DAYS GORRILLA TREKKING

over view

3-Day Gorilla Trekking in uganda gives you achance to explore into uganda's jungle for an intntimate.

Face to face encounter with the world's last surving mountain gorillas. You will be able to trekk alongside
a trained and skilled guide through rainfores ie

Bwindi_Impenetrable_
National_Park the home of mountain gorillas
The tour also includes ascenic overnight trip to relaxing lake Bunyonyi and stop overs in Mbarara and kayabwe.

Morning: Depart from Kampala and drive southwest Uganda towards Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.

Afternoon: Arrive at your lodge or campsite within the park. Relax and prepare for your gorilla trek the following day.

Accomdation: Broadbill Forest Camp (budget).
meals: lunch dinner and overnight stay

Early Morning: After a hearty breakfast, embark on your gorilla trekking adventure. You'll be assigned to a specific gorilla family and guided by experienced park rangers

TrekkingThe trek can be strenuous, as Bwindi is a dense rainforest. Be prepared for steep climbs, muddy paths, and occasional encounters with other wildlife.

Gorilla Encounter:Once you locate the gorilla family, you'll have an hour to observe these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat.

Return:After your encounter, retrace your steps back to the lodge.

Morning: Depart Bwindi and drive back to Kampala. Enjoy the scenic journey through the Ugandan countryside.

AfternoonArrive in Kampala, concluding your 3-day gorilla trekking safari.
