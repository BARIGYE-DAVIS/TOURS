10-Days Uganda Safari

This 10-days Uganda safari itinerary takes you to the most popular national parks in Uganda, including Queen Elizabeth National Park and Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. You will have the opportunity to see the Big Five, gorillas, and other wildlife.

After a warm welcome by our experienced guide at Entebbe airport, you will be transferred to your hotel. Our guide will brief you about your safari. In case you arrive in the morning, you will go for a city tour.

Overnight at Protea Hotel, Le Petit Hotel, Cassia Lodge, or Central Inn.

After breakfast, you will transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park, following the mystical Rwenzori Mountain Range. You will have an enroute game drive,

giving you an opportunity to view lion, elephant, leopard, buffalo, hyena, and a variety of antelope species.

Dinner and overnight at Mweya Safari Lodge, Parkview Lodge, Enganzi Lodge, or Katala Lodge.

This morning, with the help of our guide, you will go for a game drive, providing an opportunity to see lion, leopard, giant forest hog, cape buffalo, elephant, defassa waterbuck, Uganda Kob, topi, and bushbuck.

After lunch, enjoy a unique 2-hour boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel into Lake Edward. This cruise offers an excellent platform for photography, bird watching, and game viewing.

Dinner and overnight at Mweya Safari Lodge, Parkview Lodge, Enganzi Lodge, or Katala Lodge.

After breakfast, drive to Bwindi Impenetrable Park through the Kigezi highlands, known as the "Switzerland of Africa", with terraced farming and stunning mountain scenery before reaching Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Dinner and overnight at Buhoma Lodge, Mahogany Lodge, Haven Lodge, Bakiga Lodge, Rushaga Gorilla Camp, or Ichumbi Lodge.

Looking in the eye of the gorilla will be the highlight of the whole safari! Expect a challenging trek through the beautiful ecosystem of the park. It may involve walking a long distance in steep and muddy conditions, but it will be worthwhile as you observe the gorillas closely.

For those who prefer not to participate in the gorilla tracking, a guided forest walk or the Buhoma Village Walk is available.

Dinner and overnight at Buhoma Lodge, Mahogany Lodge, Haven Lodge, Bakiga Lodge, Rushaga Gorilla Camp, or Ichumbi Lodge.

After breakfast, transfer back to Kampala.

Overnight at Protea Hotel, Le Petit Hotel, Cassia Lodge, or Central Inn.

Transfer to the airport for your departure.
