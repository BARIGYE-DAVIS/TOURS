3 Day Murchison Falls Vaccation

Including Big 5 Sighting

Over view

This safari will enbale to explore murchison falls including game drives and boat cruises

ITINERARY

Morning: Depart Kampala early and drive north to Murchison Falls National Park.

Afternoon: Stop at Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for a rhino tracking experience. This is a great opportunity to see these magnificent creatures up close.

Evening: Arrive at your lodge in Murchison Falls National Park. Check-in and relax.

Accommodation: Ikorom Safaris Lodge (budget)

Meals: lunch and Dinner.

Morning: Embark on an early morning game drive to spot various wildlife species, including elephants, giraffes, buffaloes, and potentially even lions and leopards.

Afternoon: Enjoy a boat cruise along the Nile River. As you sail, marvel at the stunning scenery and keep an eye out for hippos, crocodiles, and a variety of bird species.

Evening: Hike to the top of Murchison Falls for a breathtaking view of the powerful waterfall.

Accommodation: Ikorom Safari Lodge (budget)

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch,Dinner

Morning: Enjoy a final game drive before departing Murchison Falls.

Afternoon: Drive back to Kampala, stopping for lunch or a break as needed.

Evening: Arrive in Kampala or Entebbe in the evening, marking the end of your safari.
