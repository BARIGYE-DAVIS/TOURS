7 Days Gorilla &

Wildlife Tour to Uganda

Welcome: You will be warmly welcomed by our experienced guide at Entebbe airport.

Transfer: Enjoy a transfer to your hotel, where your guide will brief you about your safari.

City Tour: If you arrive in the morning, you can go for a city tour.

Accommodation: Overnight at Protea Hotel, Le Petit Hotel, Cassia Lodge, or Central Inn.

Morning: After breakfast, transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park, enjoying views of the mystical Rwenzori Mountain Range.

Game Drive: En route, enjoy a game drive with opportunities to view lions, elephants, leopards, buffalo, hyenas, and various antelope species.

Accommodation: Dinner and overnight at Mweya Safari Lodge, Parkview Lodge, Enganzi Lodge, or Katala Lodge.

Morning: Experience an exciting game drive with opportunities to see lions, leopards, giant forest hogs, cape buffalo, and more.

Afternoon: Enjoy a unique 2-hour boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel into Lake Edward, perfect for photography, bird watching, and game viewing.

Accommodation: Dinner and overnight at Mweya Safari Lodge, Parkview Lodge, Enganzi Lodge, or Katala Lodge.

Morning: After breakfast, drive to Bwindi Impenetrable Park through the scenic Kigezi highlands, known as the "Switzerland of Africa."

Accommodation: Dinner and overnight at Buhoma Lodge, Mahogany Lodge, Haven Lodge, Bakiga Lodge, Rushaga Gorilla Camp, or Ichumbi Lodge.

Gorilla Trekking: The highlight of your safari! Expect a challenging hike in steep and muddy conditions before encountering gorillas.

Alternative Activity: Those preferring not to trek can enjoy a guided forest walk or explore with the Buhoma Village Walk.

Accommodation: Dinner and overnight at Buhoma Lodge, Mahogany Lodge, Haven Lodge, Bakiga Lodge, Rushaga Gorilla Camp, or Ichumbi Lodge.

Morning: After breakfast, transfer back to Kampala.

Accommodation: Overnight at Protea Hotel, Le Petit Hotel, Cassia Lodge, or Central Inn.

Transfer: Enjoy your final transfer to Entebbe airport for departure.
