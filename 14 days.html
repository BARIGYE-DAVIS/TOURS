14 Days Safari in Uganda

Over view

This 14-day safari takes you on an adventure through Uganda's most popular national parks and attractions. From the savannah plains of Murchison Falls to the dense forests of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, you will have the opportunity to see a wide variety of wildlife and experience the rich culture of the country. Highlights of the trip include game drives, boat cruises, chimpanzee tracking, and gorilla trekking. This safari is perfect for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts who want to explore the best of Uganda in two weeks.

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport where you will be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Enjoy a welcome dinner and briefing about your upcoming adventure.

After breakfast, drive to Murchison Falls National Park by pass the Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary for a guided walk to see rhinos. Continue to the park, arriving in the late afternoon check into accomadation and relax as you prepare for the next day. .

Enjoy an early morning game drive to see lions, elephants, and other wildlife return to your accomadation for Lunch. In the afternoon, take a boat cruise to the base of Murchison Falls, where you can see hippos, crocodiles, and various bird species.

After breakfast, transfer to Kibale Forest National Park. Enjoy the scenic drive through the countryside and arrive at your lodge in the late afternoon.

In the morning, go chimpanzee tracking in Kibale Forest. In the afternoon, take a guided walk in Bigodi Swamp to see various bird species and other wildlife.

After breakfast, transfer to Queen Elizabeth National Park. Enjoy evening game drive as you will be able to various wildlife including zebras knobbs, elephants, buffalo among others route to your lodge.

Enjoy an early morning game drive to see lions, elephants, and other wildlife. In the afternoon, take a boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel to see hippos, crocodiles, and various bird species

After breakfast, transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. Enjoy the scenic drive through the countryside and arrive at your lodge in the late afternoon.

In the morning, you will gather at the park headquarters for briefing and gorilla family allocation, and proceed straight into the forest to search for the gentle giants. You will be allowed one hour to stay in the presence of the gorillas, as you observe and take photographs. In the evening, you may go for a cultural walk to the Batwa pygmy village $25p/p (optional)to learn about their culture. Return to your accommodation for dinner and overnight

After breakfast, transfer to Lake Bunyonyi. Enjoy the scenic drive through the countryside and arrive at your lodge in the late afternoon. In the evening, enjoy a boat ride on the lake.

Spend the day relaxing at Lake Bunyonyi. You can take a boat ride to explore the various islands, go swimming, or take a guided nature walk around the lake.

After breakfast, transfer to Lake Mburo National Park. Enjoy a game drive en route to your lodge. Upon arrival, check-in at your lodge and have lunch. In the afternoon, take a guided nature walk to explore the park's diverse flora and fauna. In the evening, enjoy dinner at the lodge and relax under the stars.

Enjoy an early morning game drive in the park where you will be able to see wildlife ie zebra, uganda knob etc, In the afternoon, take a boat cruise (sundowncruise)on Lake Mburo to see hippos, crocodiles, and various bird species. After the boat cruise, return to the lodge for

After breakfast, transfer back to Entebbe. Depending on your flight schedule, you may have time for a city tour before being transferred to the airport for your departure flight. During the city tour, visit the Uganda Wildlife Education Centre, the Entebbe Botanical Gardens, and the local craft markets for some last-minute shopping. Enjoy a farewell lunch at a local restaurant before heading to the airport.
